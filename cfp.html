Workshop
The human foot and its role in walking:
inspiration and new perspectives for Robotics and Prosthetics
May 2025
ICRA 2025 @ Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, Georgia

Call for Papers

We are pleased to invite researchers, scholars, and practitioners to submit their original and unpublished work. We welcome submissions in, but not limited to, the following areas:

  • Insights on human foot functionality for artificial foot design
  • Modelling the human foot
  • Adaptive feet for bipedal robots
  • Adaptive prosthetic feet for bipedal robots

Submission guidelines

We accept abstracts of a maximum of two pages (bibliography excluded) from prospective authors.
The document can be formatted using the standard IEEE layout provided by the conference website and submitted at huforp.workshop@gmail.com.
All papers will be peer-reviewed, and accepted papers will be included in the workshop website. Moreover, authors of accepted papers will be asked to prepare a poster that will be presented during the workshop and a video that will be uploaded on this website.

Important dates

  • XX/XX/2025: Extended abstract submission deadline
  • XX/XX/2025: Notice of acceptance
  • XX/XX/2025: Poster presentation at the workshop
